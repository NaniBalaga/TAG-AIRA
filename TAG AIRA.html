About TAG AIRA

The Future of Luxury Living

TAG AIRA is Andhra Pradesh's first smart homes based premium villa community, offering 55 ultra-luxurious G+2 triplex villas spread across 5 acres of premium gated community space.

Our villas are designed with modern architecture and equipped with cutting-edge smart home technology to provide seamless living experience. Each villa comes with 4 BHK configuration plus a home theater and lift provision.

Prime Location

Strategic Location in Amaravati Capital Region

TAG AIRA is strategically located at Peddakakani, adjacent to Vijayawada-Guntur Highway and near Shankar Eye Hospital, placing it in the heart of the developing Amaravati Capital Region.

The location offers excellent connectivity to major business hubs, educational institutions, healthcare facilities, and entertainment zones while maintaining a peaceful residential atmosphere.
